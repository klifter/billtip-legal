Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I change the country? — Tap the country chip at the top of the main screen.
  • How do I split the bill? — Use the split stepper in the tip card to set the number of people.
  • What is BillTip Pro? — A one-time purchase that unlocks all service categories (Taxi, Hotel, Porter, Guide, Salon) and detailed tipping notes.
  • How do I restore my Pro purchase? — Go to Settings → Restore Purchase.
  • How do I change the language? — Go to Settings (gear icon) and select your preferred language.
  • How do I set my home currency? — Go to Settings → Home Currency to see converted amounts.

Contact Support
